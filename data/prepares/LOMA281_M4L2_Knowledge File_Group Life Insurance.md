Grace Period Provision
Like individual insurance policies, group policies have a grace period
provision to help keep policies in force.
Because group premiums are usually paid monthly, the grace period usually
is 31 days.
Grace Period
Image Description
If a premium isn’t paid by its due date, coverage continues throughout the
grace period. The policy terminates if the premium isn’t paid by the end of
the grace period.
When the policyholder pays a premium during the grace period, the insurer
is paid for the coverage it provided during the grace period.

Like most group life policies, The Stetson Company’s policy is yearly renewable term
(YRT) insurance. The coverage lasts for one year.
Group YRT policies do not build cash values.
The insurer can change the premium rate at the end of each policy year.

insurance coverage tax free. An employee must pay federal income taxes on the
